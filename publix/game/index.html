How to Play

Welcome to Safedle! Here's how to play:

  • You'll see two characters or tags side by side
  • The left side shows a character/tag with its post count
  • The right side shows another character/tag with a hidden post count (?)
  • Your goal: Click on the character/tag you think has MORE posts
  • If you think the left character has more posts, click on the left card
  • If you think the right character has more posts, click on the right card
  • Try to build the longest streak possible!

Scoring System:

  • Base Score: 100 points per correct answer
  • Difficulty Bonus: Easy (1x), Medium (1.5x), Hard (2.5x)
  • Speed Bonus: Answer faster for up to 50% bonus points
  • Streak Bonus: Consecutive correct answers boost your score
  • Challenge Bonus: Similar post counts = harder guess = more points

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: Popular characters (20,000+ posts)
  • Medium: Moderate characters (5,000-50,000 posts)
  • Hard: Obscure characters (0-10,000 posts)

Good luck and have fun!

Franchise Filter

What does "Include franchises" mean?

This toggle controls whether the game includes broad franchise tags or focuses on specific characters.

When enabled (default):

  • Shows both individual characters and franchise tags
  • Examples: "Pokémon", "Naruto", "Genshin Impact"

When disabled:

  • Shows only specific individual characters
  • Examples: "Pikachu", "Mikasa Ackerman", "Hatsune Miku"
